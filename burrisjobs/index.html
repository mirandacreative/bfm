Career Opportunites

Burris Logistics is a family owned company that has been in business since the 1920’s. We have experienced tremendous growth and success over the years. We have 14 refrigerated facilities in 8 States and are dedicated to excellence and customer service. We have a unique culture that values the efforts of all of our team members. We offer excellent benefits and competitive wages and an outstanding work environment.
